How to Use AMICA
AMICA is a friendly, private assistant just for you. She lives on this little device nearby — not on the internet. No one else can see what you talk about.
1
Tap "Talk to AMICA" on the main screen to start a conversation.
2
Type what you want to say — or tap the 🎤 Speak button and just talk.
3
AMICA will reply. Tap 🔊 Read aloud if you'd like her to read it to you.
4
You can ask her anything — about your medicines, who is visiting, what day something is, or just for a chat.
5
AMICA remembers things about you — family, medicines, appointments. Tap "What AMICA knows" to see it all.
Things you can ask:

"Do I have anything on this week?"
"What tablets do I take in the morning?"
"When did Sarah last visit?"
"Can you remind me what Tom does for work?"
"I'm not sure if I took my tablets today"
